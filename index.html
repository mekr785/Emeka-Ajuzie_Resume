01 — Frontal Lobe Ramblings

Simple ideas told well,

§ About

I'm a Creative Director building culturally sharp, insight-driven campaigns that move people and brands.

I cannot stand the ordinary; turning human truths into simple, scalable ideas that reverberate across film, digital, and lived experience is my north star. From Lagos to the wider world, I build stories worth telling twice.

Currently leading creative at DDB Lagos. Previously at Tequila Nigeria, Publicis Groupe (Insight Redefini), and Synthesis. Trained as a sociologist. Still curious about why people do what they do.

§ Notable Films
Pitch Win
Interswitch Brand Campaign

"Never Stop"

Progress doesn't replace tradition; it evolves it. A repositioning that kept Interswitch future-facing while staying emotionally rooted in Nigerian culture.
Quickteller 360° Campaign

"Run-It"

Nigerian youth don't wait for permission; they're go-getters. We reframed a financial services app as an enabler of ambition. Lifestyle, not utility.
MoMo PSB · MTN Integrated Launch

Better than cash.

Cash in Nigeria is often more burden than benefit. We positioned MoMo PSB as the versatile, reliable alternative for the queue at the ATM, the change at the vendor, the anxiety of the market.
Pitch Win
Betano Cultural Activation

"Make we bet."

Betting in Nigeria is emotional, ritualistic, deeply social. We turned everyday human behaviours into playful metaphors for odds, instinct, and the hunch you can't shake.
